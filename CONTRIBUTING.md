# Contributing to VaultPlayer

Thank you for strengthening the flagship media player of the VaultWares ecosystem.

## Mandatory Steps

- Follow Brand Guidelines and central knowledge system
- All new filters/effects must be local-first and encrypted
- Integrate with Google ADK team coordination for complex tasks
- Maintain bilingual support (EN/FR)
- Test real-time performance and agent handoffs

See [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for community standards.
